self hosted app directory

Search 120+ real self-hostable apps offline — by tag or free-text. No deps, no account.

What it does

  • 120+ curated self-hosted apps (dns, vpn, media, dev, ai, monitoring...)
  • Filter by tag: python3 sh.py search vpn
  • Free-text search: python3 sh.py search password
  • List all tags: python3 sh.py tags
  • Random pick: python3 sh.py random
  • Pure stdlib Python, one file, works offline
  • Perfect companion to your self-hosting journey
